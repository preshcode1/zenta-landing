What is ZENTA?

Zenta is a global esports brand built for serious competitors. We host high-level tournaments across the biggest titles in mobile and console gaming — including Free Fire, Call of Duty Mobile, and Fortnite, with more on the way. This isn't casual play. Zenta is built for players who want to win, grow, and earn. We focus on skill-based matchups, real rewards, and building a platform where competitive gaming is taken seriously. If you're here to play safe, stay comfortable, or chase easy wins — you're in the wrong place. Zenta is for the bold. The focused. The ones chasing more. We’re not here to blend in. We’re here to dominate.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a tournament?

Join our Discord and check the tournaments channel. Registration links, rules, and schedules are posted there and on our upcoming web dashboard.

Is ZENTA free to join?

Yes! Participation in most tournaments is free. Premium events may require an entry fee, but there are always free competitions available.

What prizes can I win?

Winners can earn real cash, crypto rewards, exclusive digital items, and more. Prize pools vary by tournament.

Which regions do you support?

We focus on all countries, and players from anywhere are welcome. Some tournaments may have region locks for fairness.

How can I get involved as a creator or sponsor?

Apply for our Creator Program or contact us via Discord for sponsorship opportunities and partnerships.
