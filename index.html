Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a tournament?

Join our Discord and check the tournaments channel. Registration links, rules, and schedules are posted there and on our upcoming web dashboard.

Is ZENTA free to join?

Yes! Participation in most tournaments is free. Premium events may require an entry fee, but there are always free competitions available.

What prizes can I win?

Winners can earn real cash, crypto rewards, exclusive digital items, and more. Prize pools vary by tournament.

Which regions do you support?

We focus on Africa, Asia, and the global South, but players from anywhere are welcome. Some tournaments may have region locks for fairness.

How can I get involved as a creator or sponsor?

Apply for our Creator Program or contact us via Discord for sponsorship opportunities and partnerships.
